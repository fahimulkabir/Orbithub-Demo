Gaps we are covering

Open source projects, while valuable and impactful, often face several challenges or gaps:

Sustainability

Many open source projects struggle to maintain consistent development and support over time. This can result inconsistincy.

Documentation

Inadequate documentation can make it challenging for newcomers to understand and contribute to projects effectively.

User-Friendliness

Some open source software may not be as user-friendly as commercial alternatives, limiting adoption.

Community Management

Maintaining a positive and productive community can be challenging, as conflicts may arise among contributors.
